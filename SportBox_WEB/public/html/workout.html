FAQ

How to choose rubber loops for warm-up before training?

A rubber-loop warm-up is a great extension of a regular warm-up, because by adding a small load, it allows you to better prepare the muscles, ligaments and tendons for the upcoming work. Since it is a question of warm-up, the resistance of the loop should be small, and for this purpose the best suited red and, in more rare cases, when it comes to large muscle groups, black loops.

How to choose rubber loops for muscle pumping?

In fact-this question is the easiest, because it does not matter what kind of loop you take at the beginning. The main thing-over time to increase the number of repetitions with the selected loop or move to a more powerful. For example, 20 repetitions in heavy exercises (for example, complicated pull-UPS or exits by force) and 50 repetitions in lighter ones (push-UPS on bars and the like).

How to choose a rubber loop for training gymnastic elements?

When it comes to the study of advanced gymnastic exercises, such as the front or rear vise, horizon, flag, you need to start with the loop that will allow you to hold a static position for 50-60 seconds. Only after you can confidently fix the body for 1 minute, only then you can move to a weaker loop.

Rubber loop drill


Every year for sports come up with more and more different devices: fitness equipment, fitness tapes, weights, expanders, "iron" and others. Today, rubber loops are increasingly used for training.


Classes with sports equipment, based on the effect of "stretching and squeezing", have not been new. Even in Stalin's times for training athletes used expanders and harnesses, but over time, scientists have developed a more versatile accessory consisting of 100% latex – rubber loops.

What sports are suitable for such training?

A popular accessory is in demand in almost all kinds of sports: bodybuilding, fitness, gymnastics, athletics – not all. Training with rubber loops is a great solution for people who constantly maintain themselves in good physical shape. This equipment is convenient to use in the gym, at home and during training on the street.
